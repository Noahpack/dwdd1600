About Us

History

Our founder and CEO Paul Cheney has always been passionate about seeing the beauties of the world and the adventure it holds. In all of his wild travels, Paul only wished for one thing, to be able to share these experiences with others. Through the passion of adventure, Paul started Budget Vacations as a way to provide people their own stories! One thing that he wish he never had to do, is spend time researching the best and cheapest forms of travel. He now works to make traveling for others simple and easy, taking away the stress of planning.

Mission Statememt

Here at Budget Vacations, our mission statement is simple. Allow you and your family/friends to enjoy the beauties and wonders of the world, planning free! We understand that you may not have the time or resources to find the most affordable and suitable vacations, and we're here to make that part go away. Our company is not only passionate about making planning for you as simple as a click, but to also experience these amazing places ourselves. We allow for our own employees to travel and create these plans for the customer, and be able to enjoy them as well. When we tell you about the packages and destinations that we have planned for you, this is from our own experience! We hope that you are satisfied with our work, and we will continue to create meaningful travelling plans from our own experiences, and make them yours!
