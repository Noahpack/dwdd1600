What if Im not sure?

• Comfortablity with gym equipment
are you comfortable lifting heavy weight with dumbells or machines?
• How long have you been working out?
From our experience, we would say that a beginner is 0-3 months, intermediate from 3-6 months, and experienced 6 months to a year, however some people advance faster or slower than others.
• Past experiences/natural athleticism
Some people are born with natural athleticsm and others are not, which is totally fine! The gym is a place for everyone to grow.
Depending on if you played sports or other physical activities, you may feel comfortable with more advanced workouts.
